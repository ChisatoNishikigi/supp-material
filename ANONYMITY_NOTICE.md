
# Anonymity Notice

This repository is provided as anonymous supplementary material for double-blind review.

To preserve anonymity, identifying information has been removed or anonymized where appropriate. This includes author names, affiliations, personal identifiers, and non-essential identifying details.

Some materials are provided in metadata or source-reference form rather than as full redistributed content. This design is intended to support review while respecting anonymity, source boundaries, and access constraints.

A fuller public release may be considered after the review process, subject to data availability, licensing conditions, and redistribution constraints.
