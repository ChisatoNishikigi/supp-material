You are given two structured summaries of the same video:

[WITHIN SUMMARY]
{within_summary}

[CROSS SUMMARY]
{cross_summary}

- The WITHIN summary lists anomalies inside each window. "Supported" means the anomaly is real; "Refuted" means it is not.

- Treat lines that say NONE / no evidence / invalid coordinates / forbidden format as unreliable or missing evidence.

Your task:
1. Decide whether the video is REAL (natural live-action) or SYNTHETIC (generated/edited).
2. Assess how strong the evidence is that the video is SYNTHETIC, based only on the WITHIN and CROSS summaries.
3. Count how many distinct, strong pieces of evidence support the video being SYNTHETIC.

Important content rules:
- Base your judgment ONLY on information explicitly stated in the WITHIN and CROSS summaries.
- DO NOT invent or assume any objects, characters, or scene details that are not mentioned in the summaries.

- short reason #1
- short reason #2

  Do NOT count refuted, weak, or unclear items.
